Welcome the Year of the Snake!

The Chinese New Year or Spring Festival marks the beginning of the Lunar New Year and is a time of joy, tradition, and togetherness.

In 2025, we celebrate on January 29, bringing good fortune, prosperity, and happiness. Join in the festivities with family gatherings, dragon dances, fireworks, ang pao, and delicious feasts!

Celebrate the rich traditions and welcome a year of success and happiness!
