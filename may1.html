May 1 - Labor Day (Araw ng Manggagawa)

⚒️

Labor Day, celebrated on May 1, is a national holiday in the Philippines that recognizes the contributions of workers to the country's development. It is also known as Araw ng Manggagawa.

Labor Day is often marked by rallies, protests, and celebrations organized by labor unions, advocating for workers' rights, fair wages, and better working conditions. The holiday is also an opportunity for employees to take a well-deserved break and spend time with their families.
