🎄 December 25 - Christmas Day 🎄

December 25th marks the celebration of the birth of Jesus Christ, a joyous occasion celebrated by millions around the world. It’s a time for family gatherings, sharing love, and spreading happiness.

Christmas is all about tradition—exchanging gifts, enjoying delicious food, and basking in the warmth of the holiday spirit. In the Philippines, the Christmas season starts early, with the "Simbang Gabi" masses and the famous "Parol" (Christmas lanterns) lighting up homes.

The season also symbolizes hope, renewal, and goodwill to all. It’s a time to reflect on the year gone by and to welcome the new year with love and gratitude.
