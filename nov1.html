November 1 - All Saints' Day (Undas)

"A time to remember, a time to pray, a time to honor those who have gone before us."

All Saints' Day, also known as Undas in the Philippines, is observed on November 1 each year. It is a time when Filipinos visit cemeteries to pay their respects to their departed loved ones by offering prayers, lighting candles, and bringing flowers to their graves.

This holiday is deeply rooted in Filipino tradition and is considered a special occasion for families to reunite and honor their ancestors. Many Filipinos also observe November 2 as All Soulsâ€™ Day, extending the remembrance period.

During this time, public cemeteries, memorial parks, and columbaria are filled with visitors, and the government implements security and traffic measures to manage the large crowds.
