February 25 - EDSA People Power Revolution Anniversary

The EDSA People Power Revolution is commemorated every February 25 in the Philippines.

This historic movement in 1986 led to the fall of the Marcos dictatorship and the restoration of democracy. Millions of Filipinos, including civilians, religious leaders, and even soldiers, stood together along EDSA (Epifanio de los Santos Avenue) to demand freedom.

The event is recognized worldwide as one of the most successful bloodless revolutions, proving the power of unity and non-violent resistance.
