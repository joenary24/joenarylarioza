April 9 - Araw ng Kagitingan (Day of Valor)

Araw ng Kagitingan is observed every April 9 to honor the bravery and sacrifice of Filipino and American soldiers during World War II.

This day commemorates the Bataan Death March, where thousands of soldiers were forced to march over 100 kilometers under extreme conditions.

"Ang dugong dumanak noon ay dugong bumuhay sa kalayaan natin ngayon."
