August 25 - National Heroes Day (Araw ng mga Bayani)

"A nation that forgets its heroes is a nation doomed to fail."

National Heroes Day, observed on August 25, 2025, is a public holiday in the Philippines that honors the bravery and sacrifices of all Filipino heroes, both known and unknown, who fought for the nation's freedom.

Originally established to commemorate the Cry of Pugad Lawin, which marked the start of the Philippine Revolution against Spanish rule in 1896, this day now recognizes all heroes who contributed to the country's independence and democracy.

The holiday is celebrated with ceremonies, wreath-laying events, and tributes to national heroes at Libingan ng mga Bayani (Heroesâ€™ Cemetery) and other historic sites across the Philippines.
