Christmas Eve is one of the most significant celebrations in the Philippines, marking the night before Christmas Day.

Filipino families gather for Noche Buena, a festive midnight feast featuring traditional dishes such as lechon (roast pig), hamon (Christmas ham), queso de bola (edam cheese), and bibingka (rice cake).

Many Filipinos also attend Misa de Gallo, a midnight mass that commemorates the birth of Jesus Christ.
