August 21 - Ninoy Aquino Day

"The Filipino is worth dying for." - Benigno "Ninoy" Aquino Jr.

On this day, we remember the courage and sacrifice of Ninoy Aquino, who gave his life for the freedom of the Filipino people. His assassination on August 21, 1983, at the Manila International Airport (now Ninoy Aquino International Airport) ignited a movement that changed the course of history.

His death awakened a nation, leading to the People Power Revolution of 1986, which restored democracy in the Philippines.
